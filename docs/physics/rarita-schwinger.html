1. Introduction

Rarita-Schwinger field describes a spin-\(3/2\) field.

2. Velo-Zwanziger Problem

The "obvious" action for fields which

  1. have spin greater than 1
  2. are in a massive irrep of the Lorentz group
  3. have minimal coupling to electromagnetism

tend not to have hyperbolic equations of motion (i.e., violate the familiar causality relations of general relativity). This is the Velo-Zwanziger problem.

Usually the solution is either to take a massless field, or have non-minimal coupling to electromagnetism.
