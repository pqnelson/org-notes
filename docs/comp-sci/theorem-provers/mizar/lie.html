1 Overview

This is just a placeholder for my thinking about formalizing Lie algebras and Lie groups in Mizar. On the whole, it seems following Bourbaki's books may be the best outline/strategy.

2 Relevant Definitions

Mizar's library is huge, here's what I could find at a glance that's relevant.

  • polynom3 defines:
    • Polynomial of L as AlgSequence of L
  • In polyalg1, we have the following definitions
    • Algebra over F (which is both a "ring" (doubleLoopStr) and a ModuleStr over F)
    • Subalgebra
  • In vectsp_1:
    • Ring
    • Skew-Field
    • Field
    • ModuleStr
    • VectSp
  • In vectsp_2:
    • RightModStr
    • LeftModStr
    • BiModStr
