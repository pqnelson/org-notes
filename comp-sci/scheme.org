* Introduction
:PROPERTIES:
:CUSTOM_ID: h-fcdce203-230b-40cd-bf3a-d6e9a28b10d4
:END:

This is a minimal Lisp used for pedagogy. It's beautiful.

Scheme is a Lisp-1, meaning there is exactly one namespace used for
variables and functions.

* Implementations
:PROPERTIES:
:CUSTOM_ID: h-1bc216ae-f1d2-491f-949f-5e66937e0622

Since Scheme is minimal, there are many implementations. I will
enumerate just a few:

- [[https://www.scheme.com/][Chez Scheme]]
