* Puzzle
:PROPERTIES:
:CUSTOM_ID: h-ec233e02-d68f-4a9d-b5e8-060f57df0b7e
:END:

We are given the albedo of the Earth is approximately 0.30; how can we
derive this from first principles?
(Albedo is the percentage of [solar] radiation reflected by an object.)

The underlying issue here is calculations related to radiative forcing
due to greenhouse gases.
